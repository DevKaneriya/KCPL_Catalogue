Radiator Specifications Guide

Technical requirements, material specifications, and performance limits.

Core Materials

Our premium radiators feature high-grade aluminum or copper-brass cores, selected specifically for maximum thermal conductivity, durability under pressure, and weight optimization tailored to each vehicle's requirements.

Pressure Testing

Every single unit undergoes rigorous multi-stage pressure testing up to 2.5x the typical operating pressure to ensure absolute structural integrity and completely leak-free performance before leaving our manufacturing facility.

Fin Density

KCPL radiators utilize an optimized fins-per-inch (FPI) ratio, typically ranging from 14 to 22 FPI, to strike the perfect balance between maximum heat dissipation and optimal airflow resistance for the specific vehicle application.

Coolant Compatibility

Our internal tube alloys and brazing materials are engineered for universal compatibility with all major OEM-specified coolants, including OAT, HOAT, and traditional silicate-based antifreeze formulas to prevent internal corrosion.
