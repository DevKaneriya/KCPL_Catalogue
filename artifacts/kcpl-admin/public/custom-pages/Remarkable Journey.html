A Remarkable Journey

Welcome to KCPL, the leading name in the world of automotive cooling solutions. Established in 1990 as a trading company, we embarked on a remarkable journey to redefine the cooling industry. In 2007, we proudly launched KCPL as a brand, bringing our passion for cooling technology to the forefront. Our commitment to excellence led us to establish our first radiator manufacturing plant in 2019, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge machinery. This marked a significant milestone in our pursuit of delivering high-quality cooling solutions to our valued customers.
