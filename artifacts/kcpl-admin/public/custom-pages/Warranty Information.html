Comprehensive Warranty Information

Your trusted partner for quality and reliability.

1. Standard Warranty Coverage

Krishna Cooling Products Limited (KCPL) warrants that all radiators, condensers, and related cooling components are free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and service for a period of 12 months from the date of original purchase.

2. Exclusions

This warranty does not cover damages or failures resulting from:

  • Improper installation or lack of routine maintenance.
  • Use of incorrect coolants, corrosive liquids, or contaminated fluids.
  • Accident, collision, misuse, abuse, or unauthorized modifications.
  • External physical damage such as bent fins or punctured tubes.

3. Claim Procedure

To initiate a warranty claim, please contact your authorized KCPL distributor. You must present the original proof of purchase and a detailed description of the failure. Our technical team may request the defective unit for inspection to determine the root cause of the failure before approving a replacement.
